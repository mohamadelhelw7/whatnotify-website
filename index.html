Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Overview

WhatNotify is a Shopify application that enables merchants to send automated WhatsApp messages to their customers. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

  1. Merchant data: Shopify shop domain, app settings, WhatsApp session credentials, message usage counts.
  2. Customer data: Phone numbers and order details used solely to populate message templates.
  3. WhatsApp session data: Encrypted authentication credentials stored in MongoDB to maintain your connection.

3. How We Use Your Data

  1. To send automated WhatsApp messages on behalf of merchants to their customers.
  2. To maintain your WhatsApp connection across sessions.
  3. To track message usage for billing purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Customer phone numbers and order data are used exclusively to send messages you have configured. Data is transmitted to WhatsApp via the Baileys library.

5. Data Retention

  1. WhatsApp session data is retained as long as your account is active.
  2. Order data is retained for 7 days then automatically deleted.
  3. Merchant settings are deleted upon app uninstall.

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. WhatsApp credentials are stored encrypted in MongoDB Atlas. We use secret-based authentication between our internal services.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us. Uninstalling the app triggers automatic deletion of your session and settings.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at your@email.com.

Our Commitment

WhatNotify acts in good faith and in the best interests of merchants and buyers. We do not intentionally circumvent any critical platform functionality.
